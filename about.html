About This Prediction

Understanding the Prediction

The model predicts the disease risk based on your input features such as age, BMI, blood pressure, biomarkers, and lifestyle factors. The output indicates the current risk stage and provides a confidence score for the prediction.

Risk Stages Explained

Healthy: Low risk, maintain a healthy lifestyle.
At-Risk: Some risk detected, monitor your lifestyle and biomarkers.
Early-Stage: Early signs of disease, consult a healthcare professional.
Chronic: Disease detected, follow medical treatment plans.
Critical: High severity, immediate medical attention required.

Interpreting the Confidence Score

The confidence score represents the probability of the prediction. A higher percentage indicates that the model is more confident in the predicted risk stage.
