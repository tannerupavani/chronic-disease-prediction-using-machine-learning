Project Overview: Chronic Disease Prediction

This project focuses on leveraging **Explainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI)** techniques alongside powerful machine learning algorithms to improve the prediction and understanding of **Chronic Disease** outcomes. We employ ensemble methods like **XGBoost** and **Random Forest (RF)**, and the fundamental **Decision Tree (DT)**, to classify patient risk based on various health features. XGBoost offers high predictive accuracy for complex medical data. RF improves robustness and generalization. The use of DT provides inherent interpretability. Crucially, the XAI component ensures that predictions are not opaque, allowing medical professionals to understand the contributing factors behind a high-risk classification, thereby increasing trust in the AI system and aiding in informed clinical decision-making.
